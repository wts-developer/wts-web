Financial Information

What WTS scholarship support is available?
  • MATS: dollar-for-dollar match on outside support, up to $5,000.
  • MAC: dollar-for-dollar match on outside support, up to 25% of total tuition.
  • MDiv: dollar-for-dollar match on outside support, estimated at one credit per course (about 43 courses).
  • MAR: dollar-for-dollar match on outside support, estimated at one credit per course (about 23 courses).

Scholarships are limited and require an application; eligibility does not guarantee an award. Matching support is applied to your estimate automatically.

How to Read This Estimate

Outside support is the full-program amount expected from a church, donor, or employer.

WTS matching support is calculated automatically up to the selected program's limit.

Estimated cost after support is the remaining amount the student would be responsible for under these assumptions.

Important: Matching scholarships are limited, require an application, and are not guaranteed.
